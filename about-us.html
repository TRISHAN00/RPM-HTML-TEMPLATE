About RPM

RPM has been successfully functioning since 2011. The product quality is highly recognized worldwide for providing avant-garde fire safety solutions. We are at par to meet NFPA and other International and National standards.

At RPM we specialize in Fire System Design, Equipment Supply, Installation, CCTV Surveillance, Access Control systems, Year-round Technical Support, and Maintenance Services.

In our endeavor, we have effectively completed multiple projects in industries like Pharmaceutical, Residential & Commercial sectors, Hospitals & Health Care, Power Plant, Food & Beverage, Cement Factories, Steel Mills, Plastic industry, Government & Semi-government industries, NGOs, and such.

Our innovation and efforts to ensure safety and security have garnered international recognition.

Thus, we have an expert team trained to handle different scenarios as per their specialty. By considering the customer’s budget, the pricing is affordable to provide Cost-Effective solutions.
