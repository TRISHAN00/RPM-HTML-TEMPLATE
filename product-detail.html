Fire Fighter Pump Suppliers in Bangladesh

A fire pump is a pump that is used to feed water to a fire prevention system. It is critical to understand that a fire pump does not make water; rather, it draws water from a water supply and boosts the pressure (energy) of the water. A fire pump can feed fire sprinklers, standpipes, foam systems, water spray/mist systems, or any combination of these systems and can be driven by electricity, or diesel. The input of a fire pump is connected to an external water supply, tank, or reservoir.
